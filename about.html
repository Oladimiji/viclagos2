THE COMMISSION

God commissioned this ministry to go all over the world. And we
pray that the teaching of God's word through articles, personal
testimonies, Bible Study notes and many other resource
materials we present here, will bless and change your life.

Pastors Kunle & Margaret Oyesanya.

Victory international church, Rehoboth cathedral, Lagos mainland is a parish under Victory international church (Rehoboth Cathedral) with headquarters in Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria. We are the second parish in the city of Lagos. Hence, we are referred to as Lagos2. We have the mandate to spread the gospel throughout the mainland of Lagos. We are to plant Churches throughout Lagos Mainland. Presently, Pastor & Pastor (Mrs) Kunle and Margaret Oyesanya are the Resident Pastors. They are assisted by two ordained Ministers and Pastor Peter Olatunji.

Vision

Our vision as a church is to grow and multiply, impact our community, raise victorious and prosperous
Christians that will make heaven. Our core vision is to follow the vision of our founder and the Presiding Bishop
of our Church, Bishop T.V. Adelakun and His wife, Pastor (Mrs) Dolapo Adelakun.

Bible School

The Bible School is the training arm of Victor International Church. The school is called Bible School for Discipleship and Mission. The headquarters of the school is in Ibadan with branches in all our satellite parishes. Hence, in Lagos Mainland, we are also having the Bible School. The school is for six (6) months, meeting once a week. Every Sunday after the worship service, lectures are received for 1.30hour. The next class will commence on Monday 28th October 2013.Graduation will come up in Ibadan in February 2014. And it will be a joint graduation ceremony for all our Churches. It will be conducted by our Bishop and his wife, Bishop & Mrs V.T. Adelakun
